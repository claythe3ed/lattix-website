≣ Irrigation Planning

Does your land have enough water for the crop? Aquifer sustainable yield matched to crop water requirements using FAO coefficients.

How It Works

1. Input: Farm location, crop type, acreage.
2. Analysis: The engine calculates daily crop water requirement (ETc) using FAO Penman‑Monteith coefficients, then compares it with the aquifer sustainable yield derived from our geophysical model.
3. Report: Feasibility rating, recommended drip-irrigation layout, and water budget summary.

Technical Specifications

  • • FAO crop coefficient library built in
  • • Aquifer yield model calibrated with 22 Sudanese aquifers
  • • Drip & sprinkler system recommendations
  • • Verified against IntechOpen hydrogeophysical data (2024)

Sample Deliverables

A detailed irrigation feasibility report with water requirement tables, aquifer yield graphs, and a schematic of the recommended drip network.
