How It Works

1. Input: Area of interest (coordinates, block, or concession).
2. Analysis: Gravity and magnetic anomaly interpretation, seismic line correlation, structural trap identification, and source rock maturity estimation using regional basin models.
3. Report: A prospectivity assessment with lead ranking, risk matrix, and recommended follow-up (2D/3D seismic, drilling targets).
