⊛ Soil & Crop Suitability

What should you plant? Geology, climate, and soil analysis backed by 862 research papers and a machine‑learning recommender (F1=0.80).

How It Works

1. Input: Your farm’s location and elevation (or we fetch it from our 1023‑point DEM).
2. Analysis: The engine overlays geology, soil type, rainfall, and temperature data, then runs a Random Forest model trained on 862 peer‑reviewed papers to rate suitability for crops like dates, sorghum, sesame.
3. Report: A crop suitability matrix with recommended species and expected yield class.

Technical Specifications

  • • Regional geology & soil type database
